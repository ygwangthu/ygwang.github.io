Yangang Wang is currently an associate researcher at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). Before joining in MSRA, he received his Ph.D. in 2014 from Department of Automation at Tsinghua University. His primary research area involves in computer graphics, computer vision and computational photography. His most recent research interest is markerless motion capture, including hand, body and facial capture with RGB cameras. He is the paper reviewer of SIGGRAPH / TVCG / TPAMI / CVPR / ICCV and etc.
